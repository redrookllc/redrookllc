## About

**Red Rook, LLC.** is a Tennessee-based technology company founded in **2025**, specializing in AI-powered trading intelligence platforms. Our mission is to deliver institutional-style market analytics, signal generation, and macro-intelligence to desktop workstation operators through cutting-edge machine learning, real-time data processing, and engineered technical analysis.
